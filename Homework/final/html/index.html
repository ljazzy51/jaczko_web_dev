Welcome to AVAbrew

AVAbrew is a cozy and personal coffee shop in Downtown Brooklyn. Each barista takes great pride in not only their service but also the personl connection we all strive to form with our customers. "Come for the coffee and stay for the compliments" is a frequent saying at AVAbrew. If you're looking for a coffee shop for a catch up, meeting, to get some work done, or simply just a pick me up, AVAbrew is your spot.

Barista Highlight: Chan

Chan is the heart and soul of this shop. Often referred to as mom, Chan is smart, quick-witted, caring, and funny. Chan loves reading, fashion, and music. She is always the person to go for affirmations or a pick me up.

Barista Highlight: Dawson

Dawson is our fun-loving and quirky Texan. Dawson is always one for a spirited conversation about history or sports, especially college football!

Barista Highlight: Fran

Fran is our most senior worker and a vital part of our shop. You can always look forward to seeing her latest hairdo that is always as vibrant as she is. Since Fran is from Venezuela, she's always the one to help with Spanish translations!

Barista Highlight: Jacob

Jacob: barista by day, producer by night. Jacob is our newest member of the family and he is nothing but extrodanary. He is funny and sweet and on top of all of that he is a damn good barista!

Barista Highlight: Lilyanne

Lilyanne is the fountain of pep and sunshine that keeps the whole shop running. You can almost always find them chatting with the customers at the register or playing with all the puppies!

Barista Highlight: Sean

Sean is one of those people that you cant help but to love them. He is also one of the most talented people any of us know. Outside of being a barista, he is an artist, a musician, a chef, and also a bike expert!
