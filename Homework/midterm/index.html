Welcome to AVAbrew

AVAbrew is a cozy and personal coffee shop in Downtown Brooklyn. Each barista takes great pride in not only their service but also the personl connection we all strive to form with our customers. "Come for the coffee and stay for the compliments" is a frequent saying at AVAbrew. If you're looking for a coffee shop for a catch up, meeting, to get some work done, or simply just a pick me up, AVAbrew is your spot.
